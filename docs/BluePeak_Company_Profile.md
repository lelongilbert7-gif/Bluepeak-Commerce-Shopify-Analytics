# BluePeak Commerce Co — Company Profile

## Company snapshot
BluePeak Commerce Co is a simulated U.S.-based direct-to-consumer Shopify brand that sells consumer electronics and home-office accessories online. The assortment centers on audio, wearables, charging gear, and small workspace accessories.

## Business model
- Channel: Shopify online store only
- Geography: United States
- Average selling price: mid-market consumer accessories
- Growth strategy: paid acquisition + seasonal discounting + bundles

## Strategic tension behind Project 2
Leadership is seeing healthy top-line sales, but margin is under pressure. The management team suspects four issues:
1. Some best-selling products may be generating weak contribution profit after discounts, refunds, shipping, and payment fees.
2. Some products may have quality or expectation gaps that drive refunds.
3. Heavy promotions may be boosting revenue without protecting profitability.
4. Customer acquisition channels may not be equally profitable after returns and discounts.

## Business objective
Use BigQuery and Power BI to identify where BluePeak is losing margin and which customers, products, channels, and promotions create the most economic value.

## Core questions
- Which products have high revenue but low net contribution?
- Which SKUs or categories drive outsized refund dollars or refund rates?
- Which discount codes boost order volume but compress margin too much?
- Which customer segments and acquisition channels drive the highest profit per customer?
- Is monthly growth coming from healthy profit or from aggressive discounting?
